CDA Independence™

Inspired by the Independence Palace (Dinh Độc Lập)

Thank you for downloading CDA Independence.

This typeface was created by Cōllective Design Agency (CDA) as a tribute to one of Vietnam's most iconic architectural landmarks: Dinh Độc Lập (Independence Palace) in Ho Chi Minh City.

Designed by architect Ngô Viết Thụ, the palace stands as a remarkable expression of Vietnamese modernism — where clarity, order, symbolism, and cultural identity coexist within a distinctly contemporary form. Its façade, proportions, and architectural language continue to inspire generations of designers, architects, and creatives across Vietnam and beyond.

CDA Independence translates some of those architectural qualities into typography.

The typeface draws from the rhythm of the palace's geometric façade, the balance between openness and structure, and the belief that modern design can remain deeply connected to local culture.

Rather than looking backward, Independence looks forward — carrying the spirit of Vietnamese design into new contexts, new conversations, and new generations.
